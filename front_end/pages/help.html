Features Overview

Dashboard

Manage your budgets and expenses at a glance. Add, edit, or delete budgets and expenses directly from the dashboard.

Messaging

Communicate with other users using the built-in messaging system. View your inbox and sent messages or compose a new message.

Archived Budgets

Easily manage archived budgets. View and unarchive budgets that you no longer use actively.

Monthly Reports

Analyze your spending patterns with detailed monthly reports. Gain insights to better manage your finances.

Settings

Update your profile, change your username, or delete your account using the settings page.
